# E-commerce Sales Data Analysis

## Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on an e-commerce transaction dataset to uncover key business insights related to sales, customers, and product performance.

---

## Objective

The goal of this project is to:

* Analyze revenue trends over time
* Identify top-selling and most profitable products
* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Discover high-performing countries
* Provide business recommendations based on data

---

## Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Google Colab

---

## Data Cleaning

* Removed missing values in critical columns (CustomerID)
* Handled missing product descriptions
* Filtered out invalid transactions (negative quantity and price)
* Converted date columns to datetime format
* Removed duplicate records
* Created a new column `TotalPrice` for revenue calculation

---

## Analysis Performed

### Revenue Trend

* Analyzed monthly revenue to identify sales patterns and seasonal trends

### Top Products

* Identified products with the highest sales volume and revenue

### Country-wise Analysis

* Analyzed which countries contribute the most to overall revenue

### Customer Analysis

* Identified top customers based on total spending

---

## Key Insights

* Revenue shows fluctuations with noticeable peaks indicating seasonal demand
* A small number of products contribute significantly to total sales
* Certain countries dominate revenue generation
* A small group of customers drives a large portion of total revenue

---

## Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on top-performing products
* Expand in high-revenue countries to increase market share
* Implement customer retention strategies for high-value customers
* Optimize inventory based on seasonal demand patterns
